Teaching Philosophy

As a mathematics educator, I believe in making abstract mathematical concepts accessible through clear explanations and real-world applications. My teaching approach emphasizes:

  • Building strong foundational understanding before advancing to complex topics
  • Encouraging critical thinking and problem-solving abilities
  • Creating an inclusive learning environment where questions are encouraged
  • Using a variety of teaching methods to accommodate different learning styles
  • Connecting mathematical theory to practical applications when possible
