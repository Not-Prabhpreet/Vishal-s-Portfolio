Introduction to Sieve Theory and its Applications

Indian Institute of Science Education and Research, Thiruvananthapuram

Von Neumann's Theorem and Extension of Symmetric Operators

Indian Institute of Science Education and Research, Thiruvananthapuram

Anvesha Science Exhibition, IISER-TVM

Presented the Prime Number Theorem, discussing its history, proof outline, and significance in understanding the distribution of prime numbers.
